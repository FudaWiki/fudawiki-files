Some terminology has no clear translation, or common mistranslations.  You can find here a list of those terms as well as an English description, applicable synonymous, and common erroneous machine translations.

  • Kasu (カス, かす, kasu)
    A 1-point card in Hanafuda - Chaff. See Terms & Translations for alternative Hanafuda translations.
    Can be literally translated as dregs, scum, leftovers, junk, or others.
  • Yaku (役, やく, yaku)
    A meld or combination of cards that compromises a score.
    Sometimes mistranslated as "role"
