_hololive Holo’s Hanafuda_ (<span lang='ja'>ホロの花札</span>) is a multiplatform hanafuda video game of the [Hololive](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hololive_Production) VTuber franchise, published in 2025. It is developed and published by Gemdrops, Inc., an independent Japanese video game studio, and it is available on Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, and Windows PC (published on Steam and Stove).

The game features an original hanafuda game called Holo-Awase (a more dynamic variant of Koi-Koi), as well as [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) and [Hana-Awase](/en/hanafuda/games/hana-awase). Both single-player and cross-platform online multiplayer modes are available.

The game is available in many languages: English, Japanese, Dutch, German, French, Italian, Spanish (Spain), Indonesian, Portuguese (Brazil), Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, and Korean.
