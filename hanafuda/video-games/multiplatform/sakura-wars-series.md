_Sakura Wars_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦, *Sakura Taisen*</span>) is a series of cross-genre video games created by Oji Hiroi and owned by Sega. Its first game was released in 1996, and was followed by numerous sequels and spinoffs. The main series games combine the tactical role-playing, dating sim and visual novel genres.

The very first Sakura Wars game included [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame, as did multiple other games in the series.
