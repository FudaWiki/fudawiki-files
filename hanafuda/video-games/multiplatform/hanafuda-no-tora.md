_Hanafuda no Tora_ (<span lang='ja'>花札の虎</span>, "Tiger of Hanafuda") is a hanafuda-based roguelike deckbuilder video game for Windows and mobile, developed and published by Garuru Software Labs. The Windows version was published on Steam in 2025. It is available in Japanese only.
