_Sakura Taisen Online_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦オンライン</span>) or _Sakura Wars Online_ is a Sega Dreamcast game published in 2001 by Sega and developed by Overworks. It was an online community-based game in the [Sakura Wars series](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series), and the online services for the game were terminated in 2005.

The game featured [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.
