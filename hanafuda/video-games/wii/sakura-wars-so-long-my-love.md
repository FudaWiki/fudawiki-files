_Sakura Wars: So Long, My Love_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦V 〜さらば愛しき人よ〜</span>) is a video game for the PlayStation 2, originally published in 2005 by Red, and developed by Overworks. It is the fifth game in the main series of the [Sakura Wars franchise](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series).

In 2010 a version for the Nintendo Wii was released, and versions for both Wii and PlayStation 2 were published in North American and European/PAL markets, making it the first Sakura Wars game in those regions.

The game features [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.
