_Sakura Wars_, known in Japanese as "New Sakura Wars" (<span lang='ja'>新サクラ大戦, *Shin Sakura Taisen*</span>),  is a video game for the PlayStation 4 developed and published by Sega in 2019. It is the sixth game in the main series of the [Sakura Wars franchise](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series), and a soft reboot of that series.

The game features [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.
