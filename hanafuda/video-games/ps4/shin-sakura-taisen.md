_Sakura Wars_ (Japanese: <span lang='ja'>新サクラ大戦, *Shin Sakura Taisen*</span>, "New Sakura Wars") is a PlayStation 4 game developed and published by Sega in 2019.

It includes a [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) minigame.
