[**NewHanafuda.art**](https://newhanafuda.art/) is a website for playing [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) developed by Andre Hammons, published by FudaPop (Andre Hammons). The project was first published in 2023, and was previously known as _Let's Play! Koi-Koi_ before moving to a new website.

The main distinguishing feature of this video game is that it has a wide range of card designs to choose from. Making more hanafuda art available for people to play with was a major goal of the project.

The interface is available in multiple languages: English, Japanese, Polish, Russian and French.

# Gameplay and rules
As of early 2026 the game is single-player only, although multiplayer is planned for the future. Player rankings are available for registered users. 

New card designs can be unlocked with coins earned by playing the game.

There is an option for assistive card labels, which mark each card with the month number and a single-letter abbreviation of the card rank.

## Koi-Koi rules
The following rules are used in this game. The most distinctive features are its multipliers and Bright yaku values.
- Each time koi-koi is called, the koi-koi multiplier goes up by 1 for both players. (×2 for 1 koi-koi call, ×3 for 2 calls, ×4 for 3 calls, etc.)
- By default there is a ×2 score multiplier for reaching 7 points or more (before multipliers). It can be turned off in the game options. This multiplier stacks with the koi-koi multiplier.
- The values of Bright yaku are a little unusual[^1] at 15, 8, 7, and 6 points (for 5, 4, Rainy 4, and 3 Brights respectively).
- Boar-Deer-Butterflies and the two special Ribbon yaku are all 5 points each. (None of these earn bonus points for extra cards, and each can combine with their respective generic Animal/Ribbon yaku.)
- Viewing yaku are 5 points each. They are included by default, but there is an option to require at least one other yaku before being able to end the round, or to turn Viewing yaku off entirely.
- The special Sake Cup rule (counting as an Animal card and a Chaff card at the same time) is on by default, but can be turned off in the game options.
- The winner becomes the dealer in the next round.
