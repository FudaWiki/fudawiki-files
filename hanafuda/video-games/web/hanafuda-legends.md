**Hanafuda Legends** is a free, browser-based **Koi-Koi** (Hanafuda) game with online multiplayer, vs-computer, and private rooms. Play in English at [www.hanafudalegends.com](https://www.hanafudalegends.com/).

### Features

- **Ranked matchmaking** — 6- or 12-round games; global leaderboard.
- **Private room** — Create a game and share link or room code (no account required for guests).
- **Vs computer** — AI with selectable difficulty.
- **Achievements** — 100+ milestones (yaku collection, streaks, tier, special combos, etc.).
- **Ranking system** — YAKU points and tiers (Bronze → Diamond).
- **Custom deck themes** — Traditional deck and alternate card sets (e.g. ENERO: EL PINO).
- **Table themes** — Default, Dragon, Katana, Temple, Wood, Cherry, Solid (visual backgrounds).
- **Free** — No registration for private play; optional account for ranked and progress.

---

### Ranking system

- **YAKU** — Ranking points. New players start at 1,000. Earned/lost in ranked matches.
- **Tiers** (by YAKU): **Bronze** (0–1,000), **Silver** (1,001–1,200), **Gold** (1,201–1,500), **Platinum** (1,501–2,000), **Diamond** (2,001+).
- **Leaderboard** — Sorted by YAKU; top 100, top 10, and #1 have dedicated achievements.

---

### Achievements

Over **100 achievements** in seven categories, with rarities from Common to Mythic:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Yaku collection** | Collect specific yaku N times (e.g. Five Brights, Moon Viewing, Poetry Ribbons). |
| **Special combination** | Viewing Master, Perfect Round (3 yaku in one round), Yaku Overload (5 in one round), Complete Collection (all 12 yaku types). |
| **Progressive** | First Steps, Yaku Collector (50 total), Yaku Legend (500), Yaku Immortal (5,000). |
| **Gameplay** | First Victory, Win Streak, Century Club (100 games), Koi-Koi Master, High Roller (20 pts/round), Comeback King, Perfect Sweep (win all 12 rounds). |
| **Tier** | Bronze Warrior → Diamond Legend; Top 100, Top 10, Number One. |
| **Special event** | Lucky Draw (exactly 7 pts), Rainy Day Special, Moonlight Victory, Hiki Master (10 Hiki captures). |
| **Collection variety** | Diverse Collector, Complete Variety (all 12 types), Balanced Master. |
