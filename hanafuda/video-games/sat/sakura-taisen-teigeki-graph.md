_Sakura Taisen Teigeki Graph_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦 帝撃グラフ</span>) is a video game for the Sega Saturn, published in 1998 by Sega and developed by Red. It is a spinoff game in the [Sakura Wars series](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series).

The game features [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.
