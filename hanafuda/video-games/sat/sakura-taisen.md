_Sakura Taisen_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦</span>) or _Sakura Wars_ is a video game for the Sega Saturn and Sega Dreamcast, originally published in 1996 by Sega. It was developed by Overworks. It is the first game in the [Sakura Wars series](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series).

The game features [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.

## Remakes and Rereleases
* [Sakura Wars: In Hot Blood](/en/hanafuda/video-games/ps2/sakura-wars-in-hot-blood) (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦 熱き血潮に</span>) for the PlayStation 2 (2003)
* [Sakura Taisen 1 & 2](/en/hanafuda/video-games/psp/sakura-taisen-1-2) (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦１＆２</span>) for the PlayStation Portable (2006)
