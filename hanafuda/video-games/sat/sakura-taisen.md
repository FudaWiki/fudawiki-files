_Sakura Taisen_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦</span>) is a game for the Sega Saturn and Sega Dreamcast, originally published in 1996 by Sega. It was developed by Overworks. It is the first game in the [Sakura Wars series](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series).

The game features [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.

# Remakes and Rereleases

* [Sakura Taisen 1 & 2](/en/hanafuda/video-games/psp/sakura-taisen-1-2) (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦１＆２</span>) for the PlayStation Portable (2006)
