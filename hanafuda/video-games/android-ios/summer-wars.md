_Summer Wars \~Hanafuda Koikoi\~_ (<span lang='ja'>	サマーウォーズ～花札KOIKOI～</span>) was an iOS game published in 2010 by INDEX. It was developed by INDEX. It is discontinued, and is now no longer available. 

It was a tie-in game for the popular 2009 anime movie Summer Wars. (See also: [Hanafuda in Popular Culture](/en/hanafuda/popular-culture))
