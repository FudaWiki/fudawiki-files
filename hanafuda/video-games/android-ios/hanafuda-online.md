_Hanafuda Online_ (<span lang='ja'>花札Online</span>) is an Android/iOS game for playing [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi). It was developed by Sorairo and published in 2016. The game is only available in Japanese.

Hanafuda Online is multiplayer only. [Hanafuda Miyabi](https://fudawiki.org/e/en/hanafuda/video-games/android-ios/hanafuda-miyabi) is the single-player client by the same developer.
