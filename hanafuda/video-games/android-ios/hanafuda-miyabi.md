_Hanafuda Miyabi_ (<span lang='ja'>花札MIYABI</span>) is an Android/iOS game for playing [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi). It was developed by Sorairo and published in 2013. The game is only available in Japanese.

Hanafuda Miyabi is single-player only. [Hanafuda Online](https://fudawiki.org/e/en/hanafuda/video-games/android-ios/hanafuda-online) is the online multiplayer client by the same developer.
