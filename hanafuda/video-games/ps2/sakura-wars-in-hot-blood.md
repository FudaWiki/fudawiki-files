_Sakura Wars: In Hot Blood_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦 熱き血潮に</span>) is a video game for the PlayStation 2, published in 2003 by Red and developed by Overworks. It is a remake of the 1996 video game [Sakura Wars](https://fudawiki.org/en/hanafuda/video-games/sat/sakura-taisen), the first game in the [Sakura Wars series](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series).

The game features [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.
