_Clubhouse Games: 51 Worldwide Classics_, known simply as _51 Worldwide Games_ (<span lang='ja'>世界のアソビ大全51</span>) in Europe and Japan, is a game collection for the Nintendo Switch that includes [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi). It was published in 2020 by Nintendo, and developed by NDcube, a subsidiary of Nintendo. It is a sequel of [Clubhouse Games (a.k.a. 42 All Time Classics)]( https://fudawiki.org/e/en/hanafuda/video-games/ds/daredemo-asobi-taizen) for the Nintendo DS.

The game has both single player and multiplayer modes. It supports many languages: English (US), English (UK), Japanese, French (Europe), French (Canada), German, Italian, Spanish (Europe), Spanish (Latin America), Dutch, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese.
