_Koi-Koi Japan: Hanafuda playing cards_ (<span lang='ja'>こいこい花札</span>) is a game for the Nintendo Switch for playing [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi). It was published in 2024 by Sticky Rice Games. It is the Switch version of [Koi-Koi Japan](/en/hanafuda/video-games/steam/koi-koi-japan) for PC.

The game includes both single player and cross-platform online multiplayer modes. The game supports several languages: English (US), Japanese, French, Spanish (Latin America), Simplified Chinese. and Traditional Chinese.
