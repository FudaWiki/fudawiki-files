_Sakura Taisen 1 & 2_ (<span lang='ja'>サクラ大戦１＆２</span>) is a 2006 video game for the PlayStation Portable, published and developed by Red. It is a compilation of the Sega Saturn games [Sakura Wars](https://fudawiki.org/en/hanafuda/video-games/sat/sakura-taisen) and Sakura Wars 2: Thou Shalt Not Die, the first two games in the [Sakura Wars series](/en/hanafuda/video-games/multiplatform/sakura-wars-series). It was only released in Japan.

The game features [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) as a minigame.
