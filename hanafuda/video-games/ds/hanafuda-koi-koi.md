_Hanafuda Koi-Koi_ (<span lang='ja'>花札 こいこい</span>), also known as _NDS KoiKoi_, is a homebrew Nintendo DS game for playing [Koi-Koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi), developed by MeRAMAN and published on his website in 2010. It featured an online ranking, allowing players to upload their highscores.
