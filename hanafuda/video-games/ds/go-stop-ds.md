_Beodeul Matgo DS_ (<span lang='ja'>버들 맞고DS</span>, "Willow 2-player Go-Stop DS"), known in English as _Go Stop DS_, is a homebrew Nintendo DS game for playing 2-player [Go-Stop](/en/hanafuda/games/go-stop), developed by Beodeulpiri and published on his website in 2007.

While the title and website are in Korean, the game itself is fully in English.
