_Maido Hanafuda_ (<span lang='ja'>毎度 花札</span>) is a 3DSWare game published in 2012 by Arc System Works.
It was developed by Arc System Works.

## Rulesets
- Koi-Koi
- Hana Awase

## Notes

It has variable difficulty and adjustable card animation speed.
