_Real-Gostop VR_ (<span lang="ko">찐맞고 VR, *Jjin Matgo Beu'iareu*</span>) is a virtual reality only app for playing Matgo, i.e. 2-player [Go-Stop](/en/hanafuda/games/go-stop). It was published in 2024, and developed and published by Appnori Inc. It is available for Windows on Steam, and on Meta Quest.

It features single-player and online multiplayer modes, and a virtual avatar creator.
