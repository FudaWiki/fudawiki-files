_Value Hanafuda 2_ (<span lang='ja'>バリュー花札２</span>) is a Windows game published in 2001 by Magnolia.
It was developed by OFFICE KOUKAN.

## Rulesets
- Hiyoko
- Sudaoshi
- Oicho-Kabu
- Koi-Koi
- Hana Awase
- Hachi Hachi
