_Touhou Hanafuda: Koi-Koi Yūgi_<!--"Touhou" instead of "Tōhō", because that's the official English title of the franchise--> (<span lang='ja'>東方花札　～恋々遊戯～</span>) is a [doujin game](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doujin_soft) for Windows published in 2014 by Studio Himawari.

It is a strip [Koi-koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) game featuring Touhou characters. It uses an original [character hanafuda](https://fudawiki.org/en/hanafuda/character) deck where Animal and Bright cards are marked with a yellow background.
