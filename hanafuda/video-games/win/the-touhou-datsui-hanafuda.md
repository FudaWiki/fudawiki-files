_The Touhou Datsui Hanafuda_<!--"Touhou" instead of "Tōhō", because that's the official English title of the franchise--> (<span lang='ja'>THE 東方脱衣花札</span>, "The Touhou Strip Hanafuda")  is a [doujin game](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doujin_soft) for Windows published in 2014 by Yaoyorozu-Kobo. It is a strip [Koi-koi](/en/hanafuda/games/koi-koi) game featuring Touhou characters.
