**Kyū-Rei Hana** [九十花 or 九〇花 - きゅうれいはな, lit "Ninety Flowers"] is a [Hana-Awase](/en/hanafuda/games/hana-awase)-type game for 3 players, using no *teyaku*, only 4 *dekiyaku*, and a unique distribution of card values.
