There is some disagreement in the English community between what to call certain card types.  This page includes a list of terms, followed by a list of common english translations.

  • 光 [ひかり, hikari] (20-point cards)
    • Brights
    • Lights
  • 種 [たね, tane] (10-point cards)
    • Animals
    • Tens
    • Seeds
  • 短冊 [たんざく, tanzaku] (5-point cards)
    • Ribbons
    • Scrolls
    • Strips
  • カス [kasu] (1-point cards)
    • Chaff
    • Plains
    • Junk
    • Dregs
